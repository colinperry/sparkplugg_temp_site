THEATER

We partner with actors, theater productions, and special events that celebrate the world of theater.


Reward opportunities will include access to events, VIP meets and unique experiences with celebrities in the art community. Fans can bid and/or donate for a chance to win. Bids and donations all go to the charity tied to that reward.


From time to time, there will be competitions between celebrities to raise money for their chosen charity. Fans will have the opportunity to donate and support their favorite cause or celebrity. One fan from each group will win, with a special reward for the winner from the group that raised the most money.
