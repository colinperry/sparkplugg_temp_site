Sparkplugg’s charity partners are bringing celebrities and fans together to support the important causes they are most passionate about. Fans have the opportunity to win once in a lifetime experiences with the celebrities they love as they support that celebrity’s cause.


PROFILE

Join our community! After setting up your profile, every Sparkplugg opportunity is a click away. Search for your favorite celebrities, the causes you’re most passionate about and opportunities at live events you are attending or watching.


DONATE TO WIN

Click the donate button and you’re automatically entered for a chance to win the celebrity experience tied to that cause. If you’d like to donate more than $5 to the cause or you would like more chances to win, enter a larger dollar amount before clicking donate. Every additional $5 = 1 additional entry. (For example $100 = 20 entries).

Every fan receives a reward for their donation.


BID TO WIN

The highest bidding fan wins the celebrity experience tied to that cause. Fans can bid until the opportunity expires or they can “buy it now” and the opportunity is closed and won.


WINNER ANNOUNCED

The winner is announced shortly after the opportunity has expired. The winner is notified via the Sparkplugg website and sent instructions on claiming their prize. The winner is also announced to the audience during live event opportunities.


SHARE YOUR PASSION

Become an active part of our Sparkplugg community. Share the causes you’ve supported and the opportunities you’ve won. Experience the excitement of being part of a team that supported a cause in a huge way. Gift sparks to your friends and family - ignite their passion to make a difference.
